## 4. Career & Wealth (Highest Priority)
### Career suitability — Fact + Interpretation
Mercury rules both your 1st house (self) and 10th house (career) — a textbook Dharma-Karmadhipati combination, generally one of the strongest career indicators in Parashari astrology. Combined with an exalted Sun and 8th-house placement, this strongly favors **analytical, technical, research-heavy, or investigative fields** — which aligns very naturally with CS engineering. The 8th house specifically favors specializations like data science, backend/systems engineering, cybersecurity, AI/ML research, or forensic/security-adjacent tech, over highly public-facing roles (sales, frontline management).
### Job vs Business
Early-to-mid career, the chart favors **employment** — building deep expertise within structured organizations (Mercury near-combust + Mars in an inimical sign both suggest you benefit from a stable platform while skill-building). Partnership-based entrepreneurship (co-founding, joint ventures) becomes more plausible **after your mid-20s**, supported by your strong, exalted Venus (2nd lord) in the partnership house.
### Foreign opportunities
This is a genuine strength. Venus (exalted) conjunct Rahu in the 7th house, in Pisces (a "foreign" sign), is a classic combination for international collaborations, remote/global work, or relocation tied to partnerships. Given how globally distributed tech work already is, this is realistically very workable for you.
### Wealth accumulation
Venus exalted as 2nd house lord, placed in the 7th, suggests wealth built substantially **through partnerships/collaboration** rather than solo windfalls. Jupiter (weakened, in an inimical sign) tempers expectations of easy financial luck — gains will be steady, earned, not gifted. Saturn-Moon in the 11th (gains house) reinforces this: **slow start, but compounding**, more substantial after your early-to-mid 30s.
### Investments/property
No strong direct indicators of major property windfalls early on; favor disciplined, long-horizon investing (SIPs, index funds) over speculative bets, consistent with Saturn's influence on your gains house.
