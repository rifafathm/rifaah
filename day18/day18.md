# Day 18: Custom Skills in Claude

## Overview
I created a custom skill named `brain-dump-action-planner` in Claude. This skill takes unorganized brain dumps, transcripts, or notes and organizes them into structured dashboards with clear action items and risk profiles.

## Skill Details
* **Skill Name:** brain-dump-action-planner
* **Key Feature:** Automatically detects input types (Full Breakdown, Transcript Mode, or Merge Mode).
* **Rule:** Follows a strict "never invent" rule to ensure no data or deadlines are hallucinated.

## Testing
Tested the skill across three specific test cases:
1. A messy personal product launch brain dump.
2. A short meeting transcript with conflicting deadlines.
3. Two separate meeting notes to combine and remove duplicates.
